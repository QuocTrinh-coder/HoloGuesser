About Me

As a huge fan of daily guessing games like Loldle, Aniguessr, Narutodle and more, I was inspired to create a similar experience for the Hololive community. This game is designed to give fans something fun to play every day, as they try to guess their oshi. This is my first time working on a project of this scale, so please let me know if you encounter any bugs, mistakes, or if you have suggestions for improvements. If you'd like to support the page, you can donate through my Ko-fi link below. Your donations are greatly appreciated and will be used to maintain the server and develop future features and related games. Hope you enjoy the game !!!

Privacy Notice

At this moment, we do not collect personal data through cookies. To enhance your experience, we use a browser feature called localStorage to temporarily store data on your device, such as your game progress. This information remains on your device and is not shared with or accessible by us or any third parties. If you clear your browser’s cache, the saved progress may be lost. Additionally, data will be reset and old data from the previous day will be removed at the designated reset time and will not be saved elsewhere.
